Built for the
everyday.

Sōft started with a simple frustration: most clothing either tries too hard or doesn't try at all. We wanted clothes that disappear into your day — that feel like a second skin, not a statement.

Every piece starts from fabric. We source directly from mills across Gujarat and Tamil Nadu — cotton, linen, and soft blends that breathe, last, and get better with every wash.

We're small on purpose. Limited runs, honest prices, no hype. Just really good, really comfortable clothes made to be worn — not saved.

Natural fibres

100% cotton, linen, and responsible blends. Always breathable, always honest.

Small batches

We produce in limited quantities. No overstock, no waste, no pressure.

Made in India

Designed and produced locally, supporting craftspeople and their communities.
